About this HIT:

  • It should take about 5 minutes.

  • You will take part in an experiment involving visual perception.

  • You'll see two images from the eyes of a color blind person and a caption.

  • You will first be asked whether or not you are red-green colorblind. If you are not, a simulation will be done for you.

  • Your task is to determine which of the two images matches the caption the best.
